    Lore:
  • Fanny, also known as Fanny Wertz, hails from the Imperial Sanctuary in the Moniyan Empire. She’s the younger sister of Tigreal, another hero in the game.
  • Despite societal norms, Fanny defied expectations and joined the army. She designed her own weapons and became an exceptional fighter who can take care of herself
    Abilities:
  • Skill 1 (Tornado Strike):Deals damage to nearby enemies when she’s close to them.
  • Skill 2 (Steel Cables):Throws cables at enemies, allowing Fanny to move when she hits an obstacle. This skill is crucial for her mobility.
  • Ultimate (Cut Throat):Fanny can fly across the battlefield using her soaring blades. During flight, she deals extra damage to enemies hit and applies a stack of Prey Mark. Subsequent skill hits on marked enemy heroes restore energy

    • Gameplay Tips:
  • Master the use of Steel Cables. Grapple using walls and nearby terrain to move around the map effectively.
  • Keep an eye on your Energy bar. Fanny relies on it for her skills.
  • Practice precision—Fanny’s skill ceiling is high, but mastering her mobility pays off.
  • Remember that she lacks crowd control abilities, so focus on positioning and burst damage.
