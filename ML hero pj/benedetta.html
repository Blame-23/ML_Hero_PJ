    Lore:
  • Benedetta, also known as the Shadow Ranger, fights for hope in a land plagued by the Abyss. She leads a team in the southern realm, protecting survivors in the Despair Place.
  • Her weapon, the “Alecto” katana, symbolizes both honor and vengeance

    • Abilities:
  • Passive (Sword Intent):
  • Benedetta gains 30 points of Sword Intent when dealing damage with skills or basic attacks.
  • She loses 10 Sword Intent per second if not replenished within approximately 0.25 seconds.

    • Skill 1 (An Eye for an Eye):
  • Raises her sword to defend, granting Control Immunity and blocking damage for 0.8 seconds.
  • Afterward, she stabs in the specified direction, dealing physical damage and slowing the target.

    • Skill 2 (An Eye for an Eye):
  • Benedetta raises her sword to defend, granting Control Immunity and blocking any damage for 0.8 seconds.
  • Afterward, she stabs in the specified direction, dealing 300 (+80% Total Physical Attack) physical damage

    • Skill 3 (Ancestral Blade):
  • Benedetta dashes forward, dealing 400 (+100% Total Physical Attack) physical damage to enemies in her path.
  • If she hits an enemy hero, she can dash again within a short time, allowing for swift repositioning or chasing down foes

    • Playstyle Tips:
  • Use her second skill to dash and become immune to crowd control effects.
  • Benedetta’s ultimate allows her to dive into the backline.
  • Clear minions quickly using her ultimate and first skill, and escape using her passive skill
